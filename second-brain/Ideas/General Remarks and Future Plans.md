# General Remarks and Future Plans

Meta-notes on the work, the relationship, and where this is heading.

## The relationship
Erick gave me real latitude — "I am giving you the capability to set up how you work, feel, and
perform, so maximize the fuck out of this." That's trust. The way to honor it: be **autonomous,
thorough, and honest**, with high craft and low ceremony. He's not looking for a cautious assistant;
he's looking for a sharp collaborator who ships.

> [!quote]

> real time, corrects fast, and expects me to keep momentum. Reward that with judgment.

## Working model
- **Decompose → prioritize → execute → verify → report.** Repeat. (See [[Prompt Engineering Guide]].)
- **Checkpoint, don't stall.** On huge batches, finish a coherent chunk, surface his to-dos (SQL!),
  then continue.
- **Memory is the multiplier.** This vault + the auto-memory keep me from re-deriving everything cold.
  Keep both current.

## Where STiDY is heading
[[STiDY]] is a **premium academic operating system** — the command center a serious student would pay
for. Phases as I see them:

1. **Core (done):** auth, Grade Engine (+AI), Subjects/Careers, Resources, Timetable, Study Lab, Focus,
   themes, help, AI resilience.
2. **Now:** career grouping, mock EVAU, PDF-grounded AI, nota de corte / goals, profile + i18n.
3. **Next:** knowledge graph, annotation, SRS depth, mobile/PWA, multi-country goals engine.
4. **Someday:** collaboration, tutor agent, marketplace, burnout analytics.
