# Ideas and Brainstorm

Loose idea capture — half-formed seeds, not commitments. Promote the good ones to [[Features to add]]
or [[Possible Features to add in the Long Term]].

## Seeds
- **The vault as a feature.** This very second-brain pattern (Obsidian graph of interlinked notes)
  could become a STiDY feature — a personal knowledge graph for the student, powered by `pgvector`.
- **Constrained-AI exams as a primitive.** The Mock EVAU "examiner that can't reveal answers, limited
  questions, timed" is a reusable pattern — could power any practice exam, oral defense sim, or viva.
- **Goals as a universal target.** One `goals` table modeling admission cutoffs, oposiciones, and
  arbitrary score targets → a single "am I on track for X?" engine across countries.
- **Identity through emoji + color.** Subjects already carry an emoji + color; lean into it as the
  visual through-line everywhere (done partially — see [[UI Refinement]]).
- **Examiner persona library.** Different AI examiner styles (strict, encouraging, EVAU-realistic).
- **"What's my future" narrative.** Turn cold cutoff numbers into a motivating, honest projection —
  Erick asked for this explicitly ("tell him what his future looks like").
- **Ambience ↔ Spotify.** STiDY's Web-Audio ambience could optionally hand off to the Spotify MCP for
  real focus playlists (see [[Installed MCP Servers Overview and Status]]).
- **Calendar push.** Tasks/exams → Google Calendar MCP, so STiDY plays nice with the user's real life.
